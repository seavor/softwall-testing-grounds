Pregnant Christina Aguilera's Delivery Soundtrack: Her Own Music!

By Star Staff, June 5, 2014

(Photo by Kevin Winter/Getty Images)

Christina Aguilera is always ready to perform, even in the delivery room!

As she prepares for the arrival of a daughter this fall, a source says the singer is putting together a labor and delivery playlist full of her own songs!

“She says listening to her own music is relaxing,” says a friend. “She hopes she’ll be able to sing along and create an easier transition for the baby as it comes into the world.”

At the top on the push playlist? “What a Girl Wants,” of course!
