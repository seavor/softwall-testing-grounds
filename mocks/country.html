    American Young’s Jon Stone Victim of Motorcycle Theft

    Jon Stone’s Harley stolen from record label lot.

    Tammy Ragusa | Published: Jun 09, 2014
    American Young courtesy Curb Records, Motorcycle courtesy Jon Stone

    Chris Young wasn’t the only country star to experience a little bad luck, when he cut open his hand with a kitchen knife, as CMA Music Festival was getting underway. American Young’s Jon Stone was the victim of motorcycle theft when his 2005 Harley Road King Custom was stolen from the parking area at his Curb Records label.

    Jon posted photos of the bike on Twitter and Facebook asking anyone with information to please contact him. And while we all hoped he had fallen victim to a prank, as of Monday (June 9) the motorcycle still had not been returned.

    Keep your eyes peeled! The motorcycle’s VIN number is 1HD1FYW155Y678218, and the license number is 6ZJ044.
