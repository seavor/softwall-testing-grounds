Find Out What Keeps Triple H Mean in the June M&F!

The WWE executive says he is in better shape now than he's ever been in his career.

"A lot of my success in life I credit it to the day I walked in the gym and started training."

One of the biggest changes that the 44-year-old has made in recent years has been how he works out.
